Welcome to Bmace Construction Ltd

Bmace Construction Ltd remains a leading construction company in Nigeria with over 10 years working experience in delivering value-added civil and infrastructural projects in Nigeria.

Our Vision

  • To become a trusted construction company to our esteemed clients and in the Nigerian business sector.
  • To positively affect the construction industry through our innovative and quality services deliverance.
Our Value

At Bmace Construction Ltd, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional quality services through our team of highly skilled professionals.

We maintain a strong reputation built on integrity, ethics, and a commitment to excellence in our work environment.

Our goal is to consistently provide top-notch construction services to our valued clients across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission here at Bmace Construction Ltd is to utilize first class technologies in the design and execution of civil and infrastructural projects.

We are committed to provide high standard services and products that are fit for the purpose.

We aim at satisfying our customers by providing best value for money.

We are committed to quality and preserving the environment.

We believe that our development is strongly related to the development of our human resources through training.

We promote an environment of competitiveness amongst our employees to promote their sense of leadership and team working.

We have been known in the market as a reliable partner for the Government as well as for Private Investors and Developers.

Our Objectives

Our work quality and performance should comply with the required measures and criteria.

Protect safety and health of our employees as well as third parties.

Protect work and surrounding environment, prevent pollution and save resources.

Achieving suitable profit to ensure sustainability.

Full customer satisfaction as per specification.
